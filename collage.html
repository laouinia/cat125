Collage

"I am not a photographer"! When I was thinking about a multimedia project, I did not know what type of project I would be doing. The first media that crossed my mind was pictures. I started digging into my photo library. I found that I tend to take pictures of sunrises and sunsets the most. At that time, things were clear. I decided to create a collage with those pictures over the period of the last few years. I enjoy taking pictures of the sun to capture the light. The most interesting concept with the light/color captured in those pictures is the sun does not change color. The color of the sun has always been the same, which is the color of the light. The color change is an optical illusion resulting in our position and the position of the sun and the humidity factor.
